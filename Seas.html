About The Sea

The sea, a vast expanse of saltwater covering over 70% of the Earth's surface, is a realm of extraordinary beauty and mystery. Its rhythmic waves whisper stories of ancient tides and distant shores.

Watch this video to enter the incredible word of Sea animals

Sea Animals

In the open ocean, sleek dolphins gracefully glide alongside powerful sharks, while schools of shimmering fish move in synchronized harmony. Deep-sea environments, shrouded in darkness, harbor mysterious and alien-like creatures adapted to extreme pressure and cold temperatures. Whales, with their majestic presence, navigate the vast expanses, and sea turtles elegantly navigate both open waters and nesting beaches.
