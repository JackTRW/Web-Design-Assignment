Recycling!

What is recycling?

Recycling is a sustainable practice that involves collecting, processing, and transforming used materials into new products, preventing them from becoming waste and reducing the demand for fresh raw materials. This process helps conserve natural resources, energy, and space in landfills.

Make A difference and start recycling!

Recycle Now is a campaign in the United Kingdom that encourages and promotes recycling practices among the public.

The initiative is dedicated to raising awareness about the importance of recycling

and provides valuable information on how individuals can responsibly manage their waste.
